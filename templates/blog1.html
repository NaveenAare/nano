Useful Tips for Best Results

How to Compose an Effective Prompt

For the best results, follow these recommendations:

  • Be specific: The more detailed the description, the more accurate the result. Instead of "beautiful landscape" write "mountain landscape at sunset with a lake in the valley"
  • Specify style: Add "anime style" or "photorealistic" to get the desired aesthetic
  • Describe lighting: "Soft light", "dramatic lighting", "golden hour" create different atmospheres
  • Add details: Colors, textures, mood help the neural network understand your idea
  • Use examples: "Like Van Gogh's works" or "Studio Ghibli style" give clear reference points
