What is Nano Banana and Why Everyone is Talking About It

Nano Banana is the code name for the Gemini 2.5 Flash Image model from Google DeepMind. The model unexpectedly appeared on the LMArena platform in August 2025 and quickly took first place in the ranking of neural networks for image editing.

The main feature of Nano Banana is its ability to edit images while preserving portrait similarity and image integrity. Unlike other neural networks that regenerate the entire image, Nano Banana only changes the elements you specified in the prompt.

According to Google, in the first month of operation, Nano Banana attracted more than 10 million users and over 200 million images were created. The service is especially popular where users seek quality alternatives to blocked Western services.

Comparison by Key Parameters

Image Generation Quality

All services use the base Gemini 2.5 Flash Image model, so generation quality is generally comparable. However, there are nuances:

Google Nano Banana offers generation up to 4K resolution with optimization for multi-language prompts. The model is trained to better understand descriptions in natural language, which gives more accurate results.

Nanabanana.ai and Nanobanana2.org show excellent quality with English prompts, but for other languages often require more detailed description.

Nano-banana.ru (via Dremia) shows average quality due to additional request processing layer.

Speed and Performance

Generation speed depends on server load and geographical proximity to the user:

Google Nano Banana: 5-30 seconds — fast generation thanks to optimized servers.

Nanabanana.ai: 10-40 seconds — good speed, but increases when working through VPN.

Nano-banana.ru: 20-60 seconds — slower due to redirection through Dremia.

Nanobanana2.org: 15-45 seconds — average speed, slows down when working through VPN.

Payment Convenience for Users

Other services: Require payment in dollars, often have card problems. Workarounds needed — cryptocurrency, foreign cards, which creates additional barriers.

Technical Support

Google Nano Banana: 24/7 support in multiple languages via chat, email and Telegram. Average response time — less than 2 hours.

Other services: English support via email. Response time can reach 24-48 hours. Language barrier complicates problem solving.
