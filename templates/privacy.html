Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last Updated: September 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Nano Banana collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our AI image generation platform. By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information when you use our service:

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • Account credentials
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use our service:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on our platform
  • Images generated and prompts used
  • Feature usage and interaction data

Generated Content

We process and temporarily store:

  • Text prompts you submit
  • Images you upload for editing
  • Generated images and outputs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our AI image generation service
  • Process your requests and generate images
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our platform
  • Ensure security and prevent misuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Content Processing and AI Training

Important: We may use anonymized and aggregated data from user interactions to improve our AI models and service quality. Personal identifying information is removed from this data.

Your generated content may be processed to:

  • Deliver the requested image generation service
  • Improve our AI algorithms (using anonymized data)
  • Ensure content compliance with our policies

5. Data Storage and Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our service and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account information: Until account deletion
  • Generated images: Stored according to your account settings
  • Usage data: Typically retained for 2 years for analytics
  • Payment data: As required by financial regulations

6. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • With service providers who help operate our platform
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or prevent harm
  • In connection with a business transfer (with notice to users)

7. Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Secure payment processing through certified providers

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request information about data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your session and preferences
  • Analyze usage patterns and improve our service
  • Provide security features

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

10. Third-Party Services

Our service may integrate with third-party providers for:

  • Payment processing
  • Analytics and monitoring
  • Customer support

These providers have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

11. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to delete the information.

12. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us through our website or support channels.

We are committed to addressing your privacy concerns promptly and transparently.
