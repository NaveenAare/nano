How to Use Virtual Try-On - Step by Step Guide

Transform your online shopping experience with our AI-powered virtual try-on technology. See how clothes look on you before making a purchase, saving time and reducing returns.

1

Upload Your Photo

Click on the "Your Photo" section and upload a clear, front-facing photo of yourself. Make sure you're standing straight with good lighting for the best results. The AI works best with full-body or upper-body photos.

2

Add Outfit Images

Upload images of the outfits you want to try on. You can add up to 4 different outfit images. The first outfit slot is always free - additional slots require 3+ credits. Use clear product images for accurate results.

3

Generate Your Try-On

Click the "Generate" button and wait a few seconds. Our AI will process your images and create a realistic visualization of you wearing the selected outfits combined into one stylish look.

4

Download or Clear

Once generated, you can download your virtual try-on image for future reference or click "Clear All" to start over with new photos and outfits.

Why Use Virtual Try-On?

  • Save Time: No need to physically try on multiple outfits - see how they look instantly
  • Reduce Returns: Make confident purchase decisions by visualizing clothes on yourself first
  • Mix and Match: Experiment with different outfit combinations before buying
  • Shop from Anywhere: Try on clothes from multiple online stores without leaving home
  • Perfect Fit Visualization: See how different styles complement your body type

Best Practices for Accurate Results

To get the most realistic virtual try-on results, follow these tips:

  • Use a well-lit photo with minimal shadows
  • Stand against a plain, uncluttered background
  • Wear fitted clothing in your base photo for better outfit mapping
  • Upload high-resolution outfit images (product photos work best)
  • Ensure outfit images show the clothing clearly without models or mannequins

Credits System

  • Additional Slots (2-4): Require 3+ credits to unlock and use
