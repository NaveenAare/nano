Common Photo Quality Problems AI Can Fix

1. Blurry or Out-of-Focus Images

Problem: Camera shake, motion blur, or missed focus makes images unusable.

AI Solution: Advanced deblurring algorithms analyze the image to identify blur patterns and reconstruct sharp edges. Google Nano Banana's AI can recover details even in severely blurred photos.

2. Low Resolution / Pixelated Images

Problem: Small images from social media, screenshots, or old cameras look pixelated when enlarged.

AI Solution: Intelligent upscaling adds new pixels based on pattern recognition, increasing resolution from 720p to 4K or even 16K without blur or artifacts.

3. Grainy / Noisy Photos

Problem: Low-light photography or high ISO settings create grain and noise.

AI Solution: Noise reduction algorithms distinguish between noise and actual detail, removing grain while preserving important textures and patterns.

4. Faded or Damaged Old Photos

Problem: Vintage photos suffer from fading, scratches, stains, or color degradation.

AI Solution: Photo restoration AI can colorize black-and-white images, remove scratches, repair missing sections, and restore original vibrancy.

5. JPEG Compression Artifacts

Problem: Over-compressed images show blocky artifacts, color banding, and loss of detail.

AI Solution: Compression artifact removal smooths blocks, reconstructs gradients, and recovers lost details from heavily compressed files.

6. Poor Lighting or Exposure

Problem: Underexposed, overexposed, or poorly lit photos lack detail and color accuracy.

AI Solution: Intelligent exposure correction analyzes the scene to recover shadow details, tame highlights, and balance overall exposure.

Best Practices for AI Photo Enhancement

Start with the Highest Quality Source

AI works best when you provide the highest quality source image available. If you have the original RAW file, uncompressed PNG, or full-resolution JPEG, use that instead of a compressed or resized version.

Choose the Right Enhancement Type

Different images need different treatments. Portraits benefit from face-specific AI models. Product photos need precise, clean upscaling. Artistic images can use creative enhancement modes.

Don't Over-Enhance

More isn't always better. Excessive sharpening creates halos. Extreme noise reduction removes texture. Over-saturated colors look fake. Use AI enhancements judiciously for natural results.

Batch Process Similar Images

If you have many photos from the same source (same camera, same event), use batch processing with consistent settings. This saves time and ensures visual consistency.

Compare Before and After

Always review the enhanced image alongside the original. Zoom in to check details. Ensure the AI hasn't introduced artifacts or unnatural textures.
